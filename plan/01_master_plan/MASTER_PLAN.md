# MASTER PLAN — FlightClaim (working name)

## 1. The product in one sentence
> "Take a photo of your flight confirmation. If your flight was delayed or cancelled, we tell you exactly how much the airline owes you — and give you a ready-to-send claim letter. You keep 100% of the money."

## 2. Why this wins
- AirHelp/Flightright take 25–35% of the user's claim. Users HATE this.
- Our angle: **"Keep 100%. We are just a tool."** — this is the #1 marketing hook.
- Users don't pay until they've seen the value (eligibility result). High-intent moment.
- Laws exist for 20+ years, 85% of eligible passengers still never claim.
- Solo-buildable because we are NOT a law firm, NOT a claims manager — just software.

## 3. Business model (the freemium funnel)

### FREE tier (no signup needed to try)
- Check eligibility for ONE flight
- See estimated compensation amount
- Read a summary of your rights
- Light banner ad on results page
=> Goal: build trust, generate word of mouth

### PRO tier (€6.99 one-time unlock OR €2.99/month)
- Generate the full claim letter (PDF) in 4 languages
- Save unlimited flights / claims history
- Gmail scan: "find all my flights from last 3 years that may be eligible"
- No ads
- Priority updates when rules change
=> Goal: 3–5% of free users convert

### PRO+ tier (optional, 6 months later) — €14.99/yr
- Auto-monitor future flights (push alerts the moment delay hits 3hrs)
- Translation of airline replies
- Appeal letter templates if airline refuses

## 4. Target markets (in priority order)
1. 🇬🇧 **United Kingdom** — English, high spending, UK261 active, 6-year claim window
2. 🇩🇪 **Germany** — highest EU261 claim volume in EU
3. 🇫🇷 **France** — large market, 5-year claim window
4. 🇪🇸 **Spain** — tourism-heavy, many delayed flights
5. 🇮🇪 🇳🇱 🇮🇹 🇵🇱 — fill in later
6. 🇦🇺 **Australia** — enter when new rules pass (2026)
7. 🇺🇸 **USA** — last; mostly refunds + bumping + Montreal Convention

## 5. What we will NEVER do (legal safety)
- ❌ Never hold user's compensation money
- ❌ Never sign anything "on behalf of" the user
- ❌ Never call ourselves "lawyers" or "legal representatives"
- ❌ Never guarantee a specific outcome
- ❌ Never take a % of the user's claim
