# Australia — New Passenger Rights (2026)

## Status: Bills introduced to Parliament April 2026

### Aviation Customer Rights Charter (ACRC)
- Mandatory cash refunds for cancelled flights
- Refunds for significantly delayed flights if passenger chooses not to fly
- Airlines must publish on-time performance data
- **NO EU-style fixed compensation for delays** (government pulled back)

### Aviation Industry Ombuds Scheme
- Free, binding dispute-resolution body
- Replaces the toothless Airline Customer Advocate

## App positioning for Australia
- "Find out what the airline owes you in refunds + expenses"
- Focus on: refund eligibility + Montreal Convention expense claims + ombuds complaint letter
- Monitor this space — if pay-on-delay is added later, be first to support it

## Relevant laws today (pre-charter)
- Australian Consumer Law (ACL) — "consumer guarantees" allow refund if service fails
- Montreal Convention 1999 — for international flights
