# UK261 — Post-Brexit Rules

Full name: The Air Passenger Rights and Air Travel Organisers' Licensing (Amendment) (EU Exit) Regulations 2019
Effectively identical to EU261 but enforced by the UK Civil Aviation Authority (CAA).

## Scope
- Flights departing from a UK airport (any airline), OR
- Flights arriving in the UK on a UK or EU airline

## Amounts (in GBP)
| Distance | Amount |
|---|---|
| ≤ 1,500 km | **£220** |
| 1,500–3,500 km | **£350** |
| > 3,500 km | **£520** |

## Claim time limit: 6 YEARS (Limitation Act 1980)
This is the LONGEST in Europe — market gold mine. Tell UK users they can claim flights back to 2020.

## Enforcement body
Civil Aviation Authority (CAA) — free dispute resolution via alternative dispute-resolution (ADR) bodies: CEDR, AviationADR.

## Key difference from EU
- Currency is GBP, not EUR
- Post-Brexit, UK is NOT bound by future EU reforms — so when EU reduces thresholds in 2026, UK may keep the stricter 3h threshold → advantage for UK claimants!
